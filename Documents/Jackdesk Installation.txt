
Jackdesk Installation
Prerequisite:
  1. Create storage account
  2. Add gmail account to whitelist

Configuration:
  1. Employee registration
  2. Create Department and assign users to the department
  3. Assign triage department
  4. Enable auto-assignment and assign algorithm after confirmation from owner/admin

Gmail Installation
  1. Signin with the added channel
  2. Test both messages to and from.
